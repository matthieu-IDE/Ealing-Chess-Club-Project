Founded in 2022

Hillingdon & Ealing Schools Chess Association (HECA), founded in 2022, organises junior tournaments such as the Hillingdon & Ealing Junior Championships, a qualifying tournament for the London Junior Chess Championships, and the Delancey UK Schools Chess Challenge Qualifier.

In addition to being an active member of the West London Chess Academy, the association also arranges regular training sessions and selects teams to compete in EPSCA, SCCU, and ECF events.
We welcome inquiries from parents and chess clashes willing to assist in organising our competitions.
