Training

Play. Train. Study. Repeat

Exclusive location:      Our Lady of Lourdes Church hall, Osborn Road. Uxbridge. UB8 1UE.
              Time:                       Every Friday*
         Sections:                   Beginners / Intermediate / Advanced
                Fee:                         £5



   We run a number of training sessions every Friday at the Lady of Lourdes Church Hall.
*Beginner sessions starting at 5pm and Intermediate/Advanced sessions commencing at 6pm.
You will be able to Represent teams in Hillingdon Chess League and in the EPSCA Inter-Association Team Competitions (under-9, under-11 & under-11 girls).
Each session will comprise a mixture of lectures and Ecf graded games in which players will be assigned to groups corresponding to their playing level.
Players will be encouraged to record their moves so that matches can be analysed whereby feedback on their play and suggestions for improvement can be provided.
They are also being invited to the West London Chess Academy to assist them in reaching a higher level.
Open invitations to play league chess in the senior Hillingdon League.
The session fee is £5, which should be paid to the coach on the day.
